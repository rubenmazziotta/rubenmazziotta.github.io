The fibonacci pattern

Fibonacci was an Italian mathematician also known as Leonardo made the pattern that is today known as the fibonacci pattern.

This is an example of the fibonacci pattern.

The way a fibonacci pattern works is that the first two numbers are added (example 1+2=3) then it keeps on going like it's shown on the image.

This is an example of how the pattern should go: 1 1 2 3 5 8 13...

The number is always added up by the previous number in a fibonacci pattern.

A fibonacci pattern can go on forever until the person writing it will stop.
