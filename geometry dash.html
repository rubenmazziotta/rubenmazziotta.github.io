Welcome to the Geometry Dash page!

Geometry Dash is a kind of sequel from a game called The Impossible Game. The Impossible game was made before Geometry Dash.

To play geometry dash you have to tap/click or press the spacebar to jump and your goal is to get to the end of the level without dying.

There are two modes to complete a level. You can play on practice mode were if you die you respawn at a checkpoint and normal mode is when you have no checkpoints and you have to complete the level without diying.

You can also play the level editor mode. The level editor mode is where you can build and share your own levels and you can also see other people's levels.

When I wrote this web page the geometry dash version I was on was the 2.1 update and I'm hoping for the 2.2 update to come out.

Just to let you know you can create your own account on geometry dash! You will find account options on the settings screen on the main menu. Once you made an account you can be friends with people and you can store your data and put it on another divice.
