7 facts of noble gases

  1. There are 6 noble gases on the periodic table.

  2. The noble gases are on the 18th column of the periodic table.

  3. Noble gases are all odourless (has no smell), colourless (has no colour) and monatomic (has only one atom).

  4. The 6 noble gases are helium (He), neon (Ne), argon (Ar), krypton (Kr), xenon (Xe) and radon (Rn).

  5. Radon is a radioactive gas

  6. All of the noble gases are nonmetals.

  7. Oganesson (Og) is predicted to be a noble gas.
